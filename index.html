Safety Standards and Child Protection Policy

Our app is committed to the safety of all users, especially children. We have zero tolerance for Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE).

  • CSAE content and grooming behaviors are strictly prohibited.
  • Any violation will result in account termination and may be reported to authorities.
  • Users can report harmful activity via in-app reporting or by emailing leyon.help@gmail.com.
  • We cooperate with child protection organizations and law enforcement.

For more resources or to report CSAE, visit NCMEC CyberTipline or INHOPE.
